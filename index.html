Frequently asked

Common questions
answered directly.

We do not have a complete brand kit. Can you still proceed?

Yes. We can work from a defined color palette and typeface direction. If brand identity development is needed prior to the engagement, that is a separate service we offer — note it in your intake form and we will advise accordingly.

We have an existing template we need to work within.

We work within existing templates regularly. Submit yours with your other assets and we will design within your established framework. If we identify structural issues that could affect output quality, we will notify you before work begins.

Our project exceeds 50 slides. How is that handled?

Projects beyond Premier scope are quoted on a custom basis. Submit your brief and we will issue a tailored proposal within 24 hours.

What if we need more than two rounds of revisions?

Additional revision rounds are available at 00 per round, confirmed in writing before any additional work begins.

Can you accommodate an urgent deadline?

Rush delivery is available subject to current capacity. Note your required deadline in the intake form and we will confirm availability and any applicable rate within 24 hours.

Who provides the copy for the deck?

You provide core content and messaging. Essential clients supply finalized copy; Signature and Premier engagements include narrative structuring and light messaging refinement within the scope.
